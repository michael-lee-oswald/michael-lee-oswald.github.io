FAQ

What can I ask you to do for me?

Anything that will help you get better in school or save time to focus on your studies. As long as it's not against the law. Anything at all... Just ask!

Common requests are:

  • Finding a tutor
  • Bite-sized expert Q&A
  • Finding an internship
  • Finding a scholarship
  • Help preparing for a job interview

How does your matching algorithm work?

We use machine learning to get the context of your message and match you with the most compatible expert in the field as fast as possible. This technology allows us to answer a lot of questions fast, ensuring lower prices.

How much does it cost to use Study Concierge?

Its free to message us and request a service. Once we confirm that we can help you, we will send you the exact price of the service you requested, and only when you decide to go further, you will be charged for only the service you requested.

No hidden fees!

How can I pay and is it secure?

We support the most popular and secure payment providers including PayPal. You can pay with any major Credit/Debit card online.

If there's a way you'd like to pay that we don't yet support – just let us know, we'll gladly add it.
